Accessibility Statement

Effective Date:

The Jacksonville Insurance Lawyer directory is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to guarantee equitable access to our legal curation and educational materials.

1. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. This Website is designed to be fully conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA standards.

2. Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Ensuring high-contrast text and UI elements for readability.
  • Utilizing semantic HTML5 structures allowing compatibility with screen readers.
  • Ensuring all functionality is operable via keyboard navigation.
  • Omitting autoplaying media or flashing content that could induce seizures.

3. Feedback & Enforcement

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Jacksonville Insurance Lawyer directory. Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of our platform, there may be some pages or sections that are not yet fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact our administrative team via the Contact Page. We try to respond to feedback within 2 business days.
