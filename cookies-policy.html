Cookies Policy

Effective Date:

1. Our Privacy-First Approach

The Jacksonville Insurance Lawyer directory is committed to protecting your privacy. Unlike many web platforms, we utilize Plausible Analytics—a privacy-focused analytics solution that is entirely cookie-less. We do not use tracking cookies to monitor your behavior across other websites.

2. Essential Local Storage

While we do not use tracking cookies, our platform may utilize basic browser localStorage or sessionStorage strictly for functional purposes. This includes:

  • Saving your UI preferences (e.g., collapsed menus or dismissed notifications).
  • Ensuring the security of form submissions (such as our contact routing system).

3. Third-Party Links

When you click on a directory listing or an external legal resource, you are navigating away from our platform. Those third-party websites (including individual law firm websites) have their own cookie policies and tracking mechanisms over which we have no control.

4. Managing Your Preferences

Since we do not deploy non-essential cookies, there is no requirement for a complex cookie consent banner. However, you maintain full control over your browser's local storage and can clear it at any time through your browser's settings menu.
