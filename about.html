About Us & Our Methodology

Our Mission: To provide Florida policyholders with an elite, transparent directory of verified insurance dispute attorneys, leveling the playing field against major insurance carriers through accessible legal routing and high-authority education.

Provider Curation Logic

Our directory does not accept paid placements in exchange for altered rankings. Providers are curated based on a rigorous multi-point logic system evaluating historical trial success, peer reviews, disciplinary records via the Florida Bar, and specialized focus on bad faith insurance litigation.

Methodology

We aggregate legal provider data, cross-referencing public directory standings, client satisfaction markers, and active Florida bar statuses to assure representation quality and clinical accuracy in our educational resources.
