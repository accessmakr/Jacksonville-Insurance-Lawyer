OUR STANCE ON DATA MONETIZATION

The Jacksonville Insurance Lawyer directory has never, does not, and will never sell your personal information to third-party data brokers, marketers, or advertisers.

1. State Consumer Privacy Laws

In accordance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), and other emerging state and federal privacy regulations, residents hold the right to direct a business that sells personal information to stop selling that information.

Because we do not engage in the sale of user data, there is no need for you to opt-out of data sales on our platform. We operate a closed directory focused solely on routing legal needs to verified professionals.

2. Our Analytics

To measure the performance of our website, we use Plausible Analytics. This service is designed to be fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR. It operates without using tracking cookies and does not gather personally identifiable information (PII) or track you across devices or other websites.

3. Requesting Data Records

If you have utilized our contact forms and wish to request the deletion of your correspondence records from our encrypted servers, you may submit a verifiable request to our administrative team via the Contact Page. We will process data erasure requests within 30 days.
